Top 6 Essential Exercises

🐱 Cat-Cow Stretch

Time: 60-90 seconds

Benefits: Integrates STNR, improves posture, builds core strength

On hands and knees, alternate between arching and rounding your back slowly.

🚶 Cross-Crawl

Time: 60-90 seconds

Benefits: Integrates ATNR, improves coordination, enhances learning

Touch opposite elbow to knee while marching in place rhythmically.

⭐ Starfish

Time: 5 repetitions

Benefits: Integrates Moro, reduces anxiety, improves emotional regulation

Lie on back, spread limbs wide, then curl into ball. Repeat slowly.

🏠 Wall Push-Ups

Time: 10 repetitions

Benefits: Proprioceptive input, calms nervous system, builds strength

Stand arm's length from wall, lean in and push back slowly.

♾️ Lazy 8s

Time: 2 minutes

Benefits: Visual tracking, reading preparation, calms mind

Trace large figure-8 pattern sideways with finger, following with eyes.

🌊 Rocking

Time: 2-3 minutes

Benefits: Vestibular input, emotional regulation, sleep preparation

Gentle rhythmic rocking in chair or on floor, forward and back.
